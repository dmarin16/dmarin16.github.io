# Goal of the Project  
This project will focus on international students' mental health and the challenges they face as they adapt to a new life. While there is a lot of data and research on international students and mental health, these studies primarily focus on numerical data and complex analyses. What these sources fail to acknowledge is the emotional and individual aspects of being an international student. This project aims to bridge the gap between data and lived experiences to offer a different perspective on an already extensive field.
